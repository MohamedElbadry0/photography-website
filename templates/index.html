About Me

I’m a passionate photographer who loves capturing emotions, moments, and stories through my lens. I aim to tell visual tales that stir emotions and capture the essence of life.

Through my photography, I seek to freeze moments in time and present them with a unique perspective. Whether it's the warmth of a sunset or the candid smile of a person, my goal is to capture the story behind every frame.

My Gear

Canon M50

The Canon M50 is my go-to mirrorless camera for capturing high-quality images and videos. It’s compact yet powerful, with amazing autofocus and 4K video capabilities. Ideal for both photography and videography on the go!

Tamron 15-30mm f/2.8 G2

The Tamron 15-30mm f/2.8 G2 lens is a wide-angle zoom lens that offers incredible sharpness, even in low light conditions. It's perfect for capturing stunning landscapes and interiors with exceptional clarity.

Canon 50mm f/1.8

The Canon 50mm f/1.8 is my favorite prime lens for portrait photography. With its fast aperture, I capture beautifully blurred backgrounds and sharp, crisp details on my subjects. A must-have for any photographer!

KF Concept Tripod

The KF Concept Tripod is lightweight, sturdy, and super versatile. I use it for long-exposure shots, low-angle photography, and steady videography. It’s a must-have for anyone serious about their photography.

COMICA Vimo C3

The Comica Vimo C3 wireless lavalier microphone system features a cutting-edge DSP noise cancellation chip, which can effectively eliminate noise in the environment; 48Khz/24bit high sampling rate super CD sound quality, the sound quality is purer and better.
